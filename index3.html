About Us

Learn more about our company and our mission:

At Home Grain Industry (KE) Ltd, we are more than just a company- we are committed toquality , innovation and agricultural excellence. We have consistently been a trusted source of a wide range of agricultural products, serving both our local and international clients based in Kenya and Tanzania with dedication and integrity.

Our core focus is on agricultural produce, and we take immense pride in delivering premium quality products to our customers. Whether you need the finest Maize Flour, Wheat Flour, Vegetable Oil, Chicken Feeds, Rice, Sugar, or other agricultural commodities, we are your dependable partner. We understand the significance of quality in the food supply chain, and we go above and beyond to ensure that our products meet the highest industry standards.

Home Grain Industry (KE) Ltd is committed to advancing agricultural practices that are both environmentally sustainable and technologically innovative. We invest in modern farming techniques, machinery, and research to ensure the production of high-quality, safe, and nutritious agricultural products

Our Mission

Learn more about our company and our mission:

Provide Quality and Diverse Agricultural Products: We are dedicated to supplying the finest agricultural products, including Maize Flour, Wheat Flour, Vegetable Oil, Chicken Feeds, Rice, Sugar, and more, meeting the needs of our diverse customer base..

We believe in sustainable practices and strive to make a positive impact on the environment and the community. Thank you for choosing us and supporting our mission.

Our Vission

Learn more about our company and our mission:

Sustainably and innovation: We are committed to advancing sustainable farming practices and technological innovation, ensuring responsible sourcing and processing to minimize our environmental impact.

Enhance Global Food Security: We work tirelessly to contribute to global food security by producing safe, nutritious and accessible food products for communities worldwide.

Empower Farmers and Foster Economic Growth: Our mission includes empowering farmers through knowledge sharing, access to resources and fair partnerships, contributing to the growth of the agricultural sector and local economies.
